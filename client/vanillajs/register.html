Sample Donate Page

This page gives a sample of how we can integrate with Stripe with the bare minimum of information. We need an email address for the customer, the frequency of donation and the amount of the donation

I have also included an example choice of what the donation might be for, linked to a product item in Stripe.

This page gets the minimum necessary to create a customer and create a payment intent (for one-off or monthly subscription) in Stripe. On the next page, the payment intent client id is used to mount the card element to capture card data

Things to consider:

  • What data do we need to capture at this point to create the customer and product / payment information in Stripe?
  • If this is a returning customer should we look for a customer record already in Stripe - maybe find by email address?
